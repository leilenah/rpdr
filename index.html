You're a Winner, Baby Random Variable

Notation: $X \sim$ Win$(d, r, a, g)$
Description: A boolean variable that is 1 with probability $p$ determined by $d,r,a,g$.
Parameters: $d$ - the number of season episodes that have occured.
$r$ - the number of episodes that the contestant has won.
$a$ - the number of episodes in which the contestant placed in the bottom two.
$g$ - the number of contestants left in the competition.
